Events in Bangalore

Bengaluru - A city known for its parks, is every party animal’s favourite party place thus making it the city of nightlife. Entertainment events - Concerts, Rock shows,
Night parties, Music shows, DJ Parties, unlimited alcohol parties, and Adventurous activities like rock climbing, trekking, rappelling, camping, mountaineering and
many more, are organized in the city throughout the year.

The city located in the Southern part of India is also a hub for sports events, Joy rides, and events for kids. Bengalureans love to attend parties especially weekend parties.
The residents leave no stone unturned to book tickets online for an upcoming party. No season is dry season when it comes to partying in Bengaluru.

BookMyTicket helps you explore the most happening and upcoming events in Bengaluru to plan an exciting week and make it a super awesome one. You can also filter your search by the type of event you would like to attend such as Art and Exhibitions, Plays and Comedy, Festivals, etc. Just tell us the event you would like to include in your weekend plan.
